# R2 Major Update | A Cleaner, Simpler Yield Experience

## TL;DR
- Major upgrade to the R2 website and product architecture.
- Two investment products, supporting USDC & USDT, with yields paid directly in stablecoins.
- Existing users will be seamlessly migrated and receive voucher tokens to redeem directly in the new version.
- **Season 2 will be ended tomorrow at 3:00 PM (UTC+8); the season and points system are discontinued (historical rewards remain).**
- **Old version pauses tomorrow at 3:00 PM (UTC+8); new version goes live Thursday at 3:00 PM (UTC+8).**
- A redesigned dashboard offering clearer visibility into asset performance, underlying allocation, and yield.

We’re rolling out a major upgrade to the R2 website and product architecture. This update is designed to make R2 clearer, simpler, and easier to use.

---

## What’s new

### 1️⃣ Website redesign

The R2 website has been fully refreshed with a clearer structure and cleaner visuals. All core information is now easier to understand at a glance.

### 2️⃣ Product & flow upgrade

R2 now offers two streamlined investment products, designed for different risk profiles and liquidity preferences.

- Support for USDC & USDT.
- Yields are distributed directly in stablecoins.
- Upon redemption, principal + yield are automatically sent to the original wallet.
- No extra claim steps and no additional actions required.
- From time to time, additional R2 rewards may be distributed to platform users.

The new dashboard provides users with a clearer, more transparent view of:
- Investment performance over time.
- Underlying asset allocation.
- Yield accrual and distribution.

**For existing users, positions will be migrated seamlessly.**  

**Based on your current holdings, you will receive the corresponding voucher tokens, allowing you to redeem directly in the new version without any manual intervention.**

The overall investment flow is now more transparent, consistent, and significantly easier to navigate.

### 3️⃣ Season 2 conclusion & system simplification

- Season 2 ends tomorrow at 3:00 PM (UTC+8).
- The season and points system will be discontinued going forward.
- All historical rewards remain intact and will continue to be visible in your account.

---

## Timeline
- **Tomorrow at 3:00 PM (UTC+8):** the current version of R2 will be temporarily paused.
- **Thursday at 3:00 PM (UTC+8):** the new R2 platform officially goes live.

During the upgrade period, all user funds remain safe.

This update is designed to reduce friction, simplify capital flows, and make R2 a more reliable long-term yield platform.
